Description of Target Audience

The target audience for the Star Valley Ranch Chamber of Commerce website, is the local business owners and concerend citizens of the city, Star Valley in Wyoming. The site is also targeted towards new or aspiring business owners who want to join the local chamber of commerce and be involved in the local decision making of the businesses. The site will be a useful tool to look up the rules and regulations of the local chamber of commerce, and will update the audeicne with meeting times and new rules or regulations that the the local business leaders put in place.
