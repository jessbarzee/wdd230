About Us!

Our Mission

At Bountiful Foods, our mission is to nourish and inspire our customers by providing a bountiful array of organic, farm-to-table, whole foods. As a company, we cherish the joy of gathering around a table filled with nourishing, wholesome food. Our dedication to promoting health, well-being, and a more connected world extends beyond business, encompassing a shared vision of enriching lives through delicious, organic fare

Our History

Bountiful Foods Started as a small booth at a farmers market in 2003. We used to grow small batches of crops in our own garden and wanted to share the abaundance with others. After a few years at the farmers market, we realized that it was nearly impossible to find organic, locally grown food anywhere else! We wanted to bring healtier whole foods to the people of Carlsbad, so we started working with other local farmers and began selling fresh produce out of a food truck. Eventually our food truck took off, and we expanded to another food truck, and soon, our store front. We now offer, not only fresh produce, but a wide variety of organic whole foods from all over the world. We make sure that all of our products are sourced ethically and contain the best ingredients. Bountiful Foods is now so much more than our first little booth. Come by to visit and get our famous fresh fruit smoothies!

Supporting Local Farms

Our produce is sourced from local farmers to support the local economy and to provide the best quality produce to our customers. We strive to support other local businesses and believe in building a strong community where everyone helps each other. Our local farmers are committed to farming in ethical and environmentally safe ways. We make sure that nothing goes to waste, so any produce past it's prime is turned into compost for future crops or fed to the local farm animals to keep their diet healthy and clean. We love our farmers and hope that you love our food too!
