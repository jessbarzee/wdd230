Site Purpose

The purpose of the Bountiful Foods website is to provide potential and current customers withe information regarding the products offered by Bountiful Foods. The Site will be a place that customers can place orders for specialty drinks, find contact information for the business, and learn about the business and what it stands for.

Target Audience

The target audience for Bountiful Foods will be eco-minded and health concious people who are looking for organic homegrown foods to fuel their bodies. While the target age group will be adult individuals around the ages of 20-30, Bountiful Foods is a business for everyone.
