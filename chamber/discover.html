Historic Star Valley Town Hall

The Star Valley Ranch town hall has been in operation since January of 2021. Before then, the chamber of commerce and other city officials had to rent other buildings for use of city business. The new town hall has been a great blessing to those who are able to use it, and has prodvided a place where we, as a community, can council together and make Star Valley a great place to live.

Afton Elkorn Arch

The Afton Elkhorn Arch is the largest arch in the world made entirely of elkhorns. It was first commisioned by the chamber of commerce in 1956 and was later completed in July of 1958. The arch spans four lanes on the raod, is composed of 3,011 elkhorn arches, and weighs just over 15 tons. If cost the city $2,500 to build in 1958, but not has an estimated value of over $300,000.

LDS Star Valley Temple

The Church of Jesus Christ's Star Vallet Wyoming Temple was annouced in 2011, and completed in 2016. The temple is one of th smallest in the world with one instruction room, one sealing room and one bapstiry. The Star Valley temple was also the first LDS temple built in the state of Wyoming. The temple is flanked by rolling hills and mountains and has the most peaceful grounds to walk on.

Star Valley Ranch Golf Courses

Star Valley Ranch is renoud for their expansive gold courses. They currently have two in operation, the Aspen Hills golf course and the Cedar Creek golf course. Members of the ranch association can gold at discounted rates, but anyone is welcome to come and enjoy the scenic courses.

Astoria Hot Springs

Astoria Hot Springs is onw of Wyoming's hidden gems. About an hour away from Jasckson Hole, it is a great destination to stop buy on your trips to and from Jackson Hole. The hot springs was naturally occuring and was used by anyone who could find it for years before it was bough and developed into sperate pools in the 1960s. It was recently redone in 2018 and is one of the best places to go and relax in Wyoming.
